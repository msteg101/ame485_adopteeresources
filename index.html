Our Mission

This site was created to shine a light on the emotional realities of adoption; both the beauty and the grief. Too often, adoptee voices are overlooked, misunderstood, or silenced.

My goal is to provide a space where adoptees feel seen, supported, and respected, and where non-adoptees can learn about the adoptee experience. Adoption is complex. Trying to understand the emotions of adoptees is the first step toward meaningful support

Adoption throughout Life

Adoption is not a single event; it is a lifelong emotional journey. As adoptees grow, their understanding of themselves and their story often evolves. Childhood confusion may turn into deeper identity questions in adolescence, and adulthood may bring reflection about roots, belonging, and relationships.
